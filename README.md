## Project areas I focus on

**Forecasting and predictive analytics**
I work on projects that use historical data to identify patterns and produce forecasts for business-relevant scenarios. The goal is always to make the output something people can actually use, not just something that is technically functional.

**Dashboarding and reporting**
I build dashboards with a mixed audience in mind, including people who are not necessarily data specialists. That means prioritising clarity, structure, and focus on what genuinely needs to be tracked.

**Business performance analysis**
Much of my project work involves examining operational or category-level data, identifying trends, and producing analysis that can meaningfully support business decisions.

**Machine learning practice**
I use my GitHub to document my learning progress in machine learning, covering practical exercises and structured experiments rather than polished end products. I find it more useful to keep this work visible and in progress than to wait until it is finished.
